Employee engagement

Nneoma Health and Educational Development Support Foundation offers a wide range of ways to involve your employees in your company’s philanthropic efforts. Not only does employee engagement increase your company’s philanthropic footprint, it also increases productivity and employee satisfaction, while contributing to employee retention and recruitment.

Nneoma Health and Educational Development Support Foundation’s breadth of focus areas allow us to connect with your employees, no matter their personal interests and passions. From supporting men, women, youths in training and empowering them, in medical outreach and children with scholarship support .

Nneoma Health and Educational Development Support Foundation will always bring your employees along in creating a brighter future for all.
