Education

Educational challenges are widespread in many parts of the world. With education, children are set for better future. According to UNESCO, there are many locations in the world where one out of five elementary age students are unable to attend school. Low income families living in rural areas dont have the means to send their children to school. But the problem is not just a rural one; low income children in larger cities also find themselves lacking educational opportunities as well. The obvious long-term problems include children unable to pursue their passions, read, write, or learn basic concepts about the world around them. This can lead to dangerous situations.

NNEOMA believes that education is an inherent right for all children. We are helping to build libraries, make school more accessible, provide scholarships for post-secondary education students, introduce our children to science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, and exchange programs. Fortunately, we have a number of partners that provide valuable education services, but we need more. The best investment you can make is education. We are addressing, region by region, root causes on how to increase school attendance, improve learning and outcomes for all children and unlock the door of opportunities for them that only education can do. Your gifts and donations will go far to help us continue to make great strides in educating Africa.

Our Education programs include, but not limited to, the following thematic areas:

  • Conference development and management
  • Program demonstration centers
  • Scholarships
  • Local and international exchange
  • Academic-community partnerships
  • Ethical leadership development
  • Environment and sustainable development
  • Library development
