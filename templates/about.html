About Us

Nneoma Health and Educational Development Support Foundation is a humanitarian organization conceived from the love of a mother. Its goal is to help communities in Africa succeed and lead better, more productive lives, give new opportunities to the poor, needy and impoverished Africans in areas of health, education and developmental opportunities

Health

Nneoma Health and Educational Development Support Foundation couldn’t stand the deteriorating state of healthcare in Africa, decided to commits itself to the mission of saving lives and reducing the burden of disease in Africa, increasing access to medicines in communities with the highest burden of the disease. We work with partners to prevent and treat infectious diseases such as hepatitis, malaria, tuberculosis, HIV/AIDS, and several neglected tropical diseases. Our work also widen into diabetes, hypertension and other non-communicable diseases, and we equally strive to reduce maternal and child mortality, combat chronic malnutrition, increase access to assistive technology and strengthen health systems.

Education

Education they say is the bedrock of any society, that’s why Nneoma Health and Educational Development Support Foundation envisions a continent in which all children of Africa can pursue quality education that prepares them to lead fulfilled lives and make positive change in themselves, their families and the entire community. Education is an inherent right for all children, that’s why we are helping to build libraries, make school more accessible, provide scholarships for secondary and post-secondary education students, introduce our children to science, technology, engineering, mathematics and exchange programs.

Development

Development of a community largely depends on how aware the people in that community are The biggest need is to widen the awareness in the minds of the people, the best way to do this is to empower them and make them think for themselves instead of letting them follow the old ways. Nneoma Health and Educational Development Support Foundation is a humanitarian organization who works with local communities to improve the quality of well being, health, education, living standards, water and sanitation, and simply awareness about the things that matter and will matter to the future generations.

Our History

Nneoma Health and Educational Development Support Foundation (NHEDSF)was founded in 2022 with a transformational desire to help save the lives of millions of Africans that lacks basic healthcare, education and developmental opportunities.

MISSION

The mission of Nneoma is to alleviate poverty and socioeconomic adversity in Africa through education, health and developmental programmes.

VISION

Nneoma envisions a world where every human being has the unrestricted opportunity to reach their optimum potential.

CORE VALUE

In the execution of its work, Nneoma is committed to the following core values:

  • Knowledge Development
  • Honesty
  • Compassion
  • Responsibility
  • Cultural Humility
  • Empathy
  • Neutrality
  • Independence
  • Humanity
