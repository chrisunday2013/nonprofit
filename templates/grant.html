Grantmaking

The world is facing some of the greatest humanitarian challenges of our time, and at Nneoma Health and Educational Development Support Foundation, we know that no single organization can tackle them alone. That’s why we champion working in partnership to spark, scale and sustain change for communities in Africa.

What we do

To solve today’s complex problems, we go beyond traditional humanitarian aids to support innovative approaches and drive transformational change.

Nneoma Health and Educational Development Support Foundation works on the frontlines of healthcare, education and empowerment in Africa.We deliver lifesaving supplies to meet urgent needs – and create holistic solutions for lasting, longer-term change

Our corporate and foundation partners helps us do this on a larger scale, reaching more people and creating longer, more sustainable impact.

Our approach is unique

  • Short and Long-term Solutions: We act immediately when crisis hit, but create lasting change by thinking long-term.
  • We take a comprehensive approach, looking for the connections between people and systems.
  • People Oriented and Market-Driven: We understand that communities are the best agents of their own change and local market are the best engines of long term recovery.
  • We go the extra mile and innovate to deliver immediate and scalable change.

Partner beyond the expected

Rather than presenting a traditional proposal to a business or foundation to a business or foundation as a donor, we believe that the strongest solutions emerge when we work together with you as partners in a process of co- creation. We work collaboratively with our partners to identity our respective and mutual objectives and the best strategies to drive impact for each organization involved and for the communities in which we work, live and operate. We strive to go beyond traditional aid to bring transformational change where it’s needed most.
