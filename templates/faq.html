Frequently Asked Questions

Below are list of frequently asked questions and their corresponding answers.

Nneoma Foundation will follow a strict post-donation procedure and bills for the same are duly shared.

Donations should be to Nneoma Foundation dedicated accounts only and not to any individual's personal account.

Both types of charity are beneficial to our cause.

We worked in Africa

Nneoma Health and Educational Development Support Foundation receives funding from public and private sources. Our work would not be possible without the generosity of corporations, foundations, government agencies and individuals like you.

Nneoma Health and Educational Development Support Foundation takes a holistic approach to health. We believe medical care is just one component of good health. For example, without scholarship, indigent children cannot be educated, empowering men, women, youths is essential to the health and social development of families and without clean water and nutritious food, there cannot be good health.

All staff members sent to the field, no matter the length of the assignment, receive security briefings tailored to the country where they are to work.

Nneoma Health and Educational Development Support Foundation works to the highest standards of integrity. We have a zero-tolerance approach to all forms of fraud, bribery and corruption. Our SCUML certificate which can be viewed on scuml.org helps make sure that our employees, directors, officers, consultants and other business partners respect and uphold the standards in the Code of Ethics and Conduct, as well as the legal, regulatory and donor requirements under which we operate.

The difference really starts with you. There are many ways to make a difference in the lives of people affected by poverty, war, natural disaster and disease. By making a donation, no matter the size, you are ensuring that people around the world have what they need to survive and recover. You also can follow us on social media Twitter, Instagram and spread the work about Nneoma Health and Educational Development Support Foundation and the issues that need our help.
