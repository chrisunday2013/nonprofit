Donor Privacy policy


Nneoma Health and Educational Development Support Foundation (NHEDSF) honors and thank you for your gift to benefit our work. As detailed in our Privacy Policy, if you choose to make a gift to NHEDSF, we may obtain additional information in connection with your donation, including your payment information. With respect to planned gifts, we also may collect information such as your Social Security Number, which is called National Identification Number (NIN) in Nigeria ( where required by law) and estate documentation.Your personal information will not be shared, leased or sold to other organizations for fundraising purposes, unless you have provided NHEDSF with specific permission to do so or as otherwise disclosed in our Privacy Policy. NHEDSF will not send donor mailings on behalf of other organizations. This commitment applies to information we receive from you both online and offline.

For additional information about our practices for collecting, using, disclosing, and otherwise managing the information we collect from and about you, as well as the measures we take to protect personal information and the choices you can make about our use of the information, please see our Privacy Policy or contact us by email at info@nneomafoundation.org.
