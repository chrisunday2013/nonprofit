Corporate & Foundation Partners

Collaboration is at the core of everything we do. We know that no single organization can solve the complex set of issues contributing to global poverty and suffering. That’s why we champion bigger thinking, bolder action, and partnerships that bring together expertise, ideas, tools, and resources in support of the shared goal of creating a better world.

Our partnerships are tailored to the unique objectives of each partner and the opportunities to leverage multiple assets, including financial resources, employee talent, customer engagement and more for greater global impact. We are proud of the incredible partnerships we’ve built with organizations.

As we lose ourselves in the service of others, we discover our own lives and our own happiness

If our hopes of building a better and safer world are to become more than wishful thinking, we will need the engagement of partners more than ever.

No act of kindness, no matter how small, is ever wasted

Impact Areas

Healthcare

In Africa, easy-to-treat issues like Infection, diarrhea and broken bones can be devastating. Without basic care, these minor setbacks can spiral Into major suffering.

Basic healthcare is never guaranteed, and there are far too many reasons why government healthcare systems are overwhelmed and underfunded. All of this adds up to a powerful recipe for prolonging poverty, diseases and sicknesses.

We believe every person is created equal in the eyes of God. However, not everyone is treated equally, especially when it comes to their own basic medical needs.


Education

Education is an inherent right for all children. we are helping to build libraries, make school more accessible, provide scholarships for secondary and post-secondary education students, introduce our children to science, technology, engineering, mathematics and exchange programs.

The best investment you can make is education. We are addressing community by community, root causes on how to increase school attendance, improve learning and outcomes for all children and unlock the door of opportunities for them that only education can do. Your gifts and donations will go far to help the children actualize their dreams

Youth opportunity

Today, even with the fact that the world has evolved, majority of young people living in Africa has often ongoing stress and challenges.

Nneoma Health and Educational Development Support Foundation invests in young people, connecting them to educational opportunities, job-skills training, and community-engagement projects.

Working together, we can support and empower young people so that they can lead their families and their communities towards a brighter future.


Empowering women

Millions of women are caught up in a cycle of poverty and suffering caused by terrorism, corruption, political instability and climate change.

Nneoma Health and Educational Development Support Foundation works within communities to build economic opportunities and connect women to technology, tools and resources that can make a transformative difference in their lives.

Together, we can help women adapt, overcome economic hardship, survive through crisis and strengthen their communities for the long-term.
